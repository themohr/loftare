Built to answer one question.

The platform fee math below is real. It applies to any estate operating at this revenue tier and booking through platforms rather than through a private direct channel.

What was built

Solaire—a complete estate platform.

The build started with the name. Solaire—sun, warmth, elevation. A name that could anchor a decade of guest relationships for a mountain estate.

From there: a complete visual identity, a private booking platform, a guest portal, and an owner dashboard. Every design decision was made in reference to the property type—not a template, not a theme, not a starter kit from a SaaS provider.

The booking engine handles availability, inquiry flow, and Stripe deposit collection. The guest portal gives arriving families their arrival guide, a curated local map, and a concierge request form. The owner dashboard surfaces bookings, calendar state, and a full guest history.

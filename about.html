About

Built by one person who cares about the craft.

Dennis Mohr — Loftare Studio, Denver, Colorado.

I build private booking platforms for ultra-luxury vacation estates. Not because I needed something to build—because I saw a genuine problem worth solving.

Estate owners with $5,000-per-night properties were listed on Airbnb alongside apartments. They were paying 15–20% in fees, owning no guest data, and operating with a digital presence that bore no resemblance to the caliber of what they were offering. The problem wasn’t the platform. The problem was that nothing better existed for this tier.

Loftare Studio builds that alternative. One estate at a time, one platform at a time, with the craft and intention the property deserves.
